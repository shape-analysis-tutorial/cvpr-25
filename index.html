3D Shape Analysis: From Classical Optimization to Learning-Based Matching

3D shape analysis deals with extracting information from geometric data, with applications in driving, biomedicine, and AR/VR. This tutorial covers classical shape matching methods, product graph formalisms, learning-based correspondence, spectral methods, and real-world applications. Challenges and future directions are also addressed.
